Overview

The James Bond series focuses on a fictional British Secret Service agent created in 1953 by writer Ian Fleming. Bond is known by his code number, 007, and has been featured in twelve novels and two short story collections by Fleming, as well as numerous continuation novels, comic strips, and a popular film series.

Reception

The James Bond film series has been a financial success, grossing over $7 billion to date, making it one of the highest-grossing film series. Critical reception has varied from film to film, but the franchise is generally praised for its entertainment value and iconic status in popular culture.

Main Actors

  • George Lazenby

  • Pierce Brosnan
  • Daniel Craig
