Thunderball

Released in 1965, Bond's mission is to find two NATO atomic bombs stolen by SPECTRE, which holds the world to ransom for 100 million pounds in diamonds. The search leads Bond to the Bahamas.

You Only Live Twice

Released in 1967, Bond is sent to Japan after American and Soviet manned spacecraft disappear mysteriously in orbit. Bond must find out who hijacked the crafts before the superpowers plunge into World War III.

On Her Majesty's Secret Service

Released in 1969, this film features George Lazenby as Bond, who faces Blofeld in an attempt to avert a biological warfare plot aimed at the United Kingdom's agricultural economy. Bond marries Tracy di Vicenzo, but she is killed by Blofeld's henchwoman.
